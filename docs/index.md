# About

EL & E InfiNet SolutionsPH Systems OPC is an internet connectivity provider (ICP) dedicated to delivering reliable, affordable, and high-quality internet solutions to homes, schools, commuters/transporting public, and SME businesses across the Philippines.

## Mission

To bridge the digital divide by providing affordable, reliable, and uninterrupted WiFi access to every Filipino, empowering households, businesses, students, commuters/transporting public, and small enterprises through technology.

## Vision

To become a trusted and inclusive internet connectivity provider (ICP) that enables every Filipino, everywhere—one community at a time, and to connect seamlessly to the digital world.
